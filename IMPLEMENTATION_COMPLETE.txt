METRICS EXPLAINED
================================================================================

📊 PROBABILITY
   What: Chance the market will resolve in your favor
   Range: 0-100%
   Color: Green (80%+), Yellow (60-80%), Red (<60%)
   Goal: 70%+ recommended

💰 EXPECTED VALUE
   What: Your mathematical edge on the trade
   Range: Any % (usually 0-5%)
   Goal: 2%+ (breakeven at 0%)

💵 POSITION SIZE
   What: How much to invest in this trade
   Calculated: Kelly Criterion with safety factors
   Goal: Risk-adjusted, professional sizing

📈 RISK/REWARD RATIO
   What: How much you win vs lose
   Example: 2:1 means win $2 for every $1 at risk
   Goal: 1.5:1 or higher

⭐ MARKET SCORE
   What: Overall opportunity quality (0-100)
   Factors: Probability, yield, timing, liquidity
   Goal: 60+ (trading opportunity)

💧 LIQUIDITY
   What: Market trading volume
   Importance: Higher = easier to enter/exit
   Goal: 30K+ volume minimum
